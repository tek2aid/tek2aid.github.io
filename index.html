Professional Technicians & Talented Developers

Infotech provides prompt and professional IT Support and services. Whatever your needs are, we have a team of professionals who can study and analyse your requirement and offer the best solution possible, whether it’s a small project or a large scale project.

During the first few years of its inception, the company focused on software development and providing customized software solutions to private businesses and government institutions in the country.

As the IT industry in the Maldives picked up pace, the importance of providing total solutions grew. In keeping with the pulse of the emerging market, the company ventured into sourcing hardware and other peripherals. Today infotech has diversified into many market segments and is a key player in providing solutions in these segments.
